India heat map for State Public Universities

This view aggregates NIRF state public university outcome data by state and union territory.

Method used: latest reported UG 3-year and UG 4-year outcome cycle per university, combined with graduate-count weighting.

State mapping research basis: matched from official university names, city identifiers in the NIRF list, and public institutional references.
